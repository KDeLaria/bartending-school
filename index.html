Bartender School

Your career "on the rocks"? Study up and practice what you learn with this quiz. Thirty popular drinks to know!

Instructions

When you click the "Make Me A Drink!" button, a picture and name of a random drink will be displayed on the right of the screen. A list of possible ingredients will be displayed to the left of the drink. Using the checkboxes, select the items that are need to create the drink.

If you need a hint, you can click the "Hint" button and it will tell you how many ingredients are in the drink.

If you do not know the answer, you can click the "Give up" button. The drink will be added to a list of drinks at bottom right that you need to practice.

Clicking on any of the drinks in your practice list will open a new page with a drink card you can use for reference.

Click on the "Make Me A Drink!" button again for a new drink.
